About Abuja

Abuja is the capital city of Nigeria, located in the center of the country. It became the capital in 1991, replacing Lagos. The city is known for its modern architecture, beautiful landscapes, and as the political center of Nigeria.

With a population of over 3 million people, Abuja features notable landmarks such as Aso Rock, the National Mosque, and the National Christian Centre. The city was designed and built mainly in the 1980s and is one of the wealthiest cities in Africa.
