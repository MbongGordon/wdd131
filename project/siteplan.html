G-Hinckz Integrated Farms

Website Planning Document

Site Name

G-Hinckz Integrated Farms

Why this name?

The name "G-Hinckz Integrated Farms" represents our comprehensive, integrated approach to modern farming. "Integrated" emphasizes our holistic farming practices that combine animal husbandry with sustainable agriculture. The distinctive "G-Hinckz" brand name creates a memorable identity that reflects both professionalism and our personal commitment to farming excellence. This name communicates innovation, quality, and our dedication to integrated farming solutions.

Optional Domain: ghinckzfarms.com or g-hinckz.farm

Site Purpose

The G-Hinckz Integrated Farms website serves as a comprehensive online presence for our integrated farming business. The site provides:

  • Product Information: Detailed information about our fresh pork cuts, processed meats, and breeding stock available for purchase
  • Farm Transparency: Insights into our sustainable farming practices, animal welfare standards, and quality commitment
  • Customer Connection: An easy way for customers to learn about us, view our products, and contact us for orders or inquiries
  • Brand Building: Establishing trust and credibility through our story, values, and high-quality imagery
  • Local Presence: Serving as a digital storefront for local customers seeking quality, ethically-raised pork products

Scenarios

Scenario 1

What farming practices does G-Hinckz use to ensure the quality and welfare of their pigs?

This visitor will find detailed information on our About Us page describing our animal welfare standards, feed and nutrition practices, and health management protocols. They'll see that we prioritize natural environments, quality feed, and ethical treatment.

Scenario 2

What types of pork cuts are available for bulk purchase, and how can I place a regular order?

This visitor can browse our Products page to view all available fresh cuts and processed meats with detailed descriptions. The Contact page provides multiple ways to reach us for bulk orders and establish ongoing business relationships.

Scenario 3

Does G-Hinckz sell breeding stock, and what breeds are available?

The Products page includes a dedicated Breeding Stock category with information about available gilts and boars, including breed details and age. Contact information is readily available for inquiries.

Scenario 4

Where is the farm located, and what are your operating hours for farm visits or pickup?

The Contact page provides complete location information, operating hours (Mon-Sat 8am-5pm), and an embedded map to help visitors find us easily.

Typography

We will use two primary font families from Google Fonts to create a professional, modern, and readable website:

Montserrat - Headings

Font Family: Montserrat (Google Fonts)

Usage: H1, H2, H3 headings, navigation menu, button text, section titles

Characteristics: Bold, clean, modern, professional - perfect for making statements and drawing attention

Open Sans - Body Text

Font Family: Open Sans (Google Fonts)

Usage: Paragraphs, descriptions, lists, form labels, general body content

Characteristics: Highly readable, web-optimized, friendly and approachable - ideal for longer text passages
